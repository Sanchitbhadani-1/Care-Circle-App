Everything your family needs, in one place

📋
Everyone in the loop

One caregiver logs. The whole family sees.

Whoever's closest keeps a simple log — meds, meals, mood, vitals. Everyone else, wherever they are, can open the app and instantly see how their loved one is doing. No more waiting for a phone call to know they're okay.

📈
Track health

See how they're really doing

Log medications, sleep, meals, and vitals in seconds. Watch the trends build over time, so the whole family can catch small changes early — and share a clear picture with doctors when it matters.

💬
Stay connected

Close, even from far away

Share photos, notes, and quick check-ins in one private family feed. Distant relatives stay in the loop, and your loved one always feels surrounded by the people who care about them most.

🛡️
Peace of mind

Help is always one tap away

Keep emergency contacts, allergies, and medications in one secure place, with an SOS button ready for the moments that matter most. Everything important, exactly when you need it.
